About Us

Binzagr CO-RO started its operations in 1976. Binzagr CO-RO is a hallmark of lean and efficient production as well as novel marketing practices. It produces three well known brands, namely Suntop, Suncola and Sunquick which are distributed nationwide in Saudi Arabia. The company also serves as an exporting hub to GCC and North African countries.

 

Binzagr CO-RO is a food safety (ISO 22000 and HACCP) certified company. There is special focus on the quality of raw material, hygiene, of the plant and capability development of the workforce.

 

Binzagr CO-RO employs over 300 employees and offers a professional and friendly work environment conducive to personal and professional development.
